About this site

This online resource has been put together as part of the CHEM21 project, which aims to educate and train pharmaceutical chemists to incorporate green chemistry into their everyday practice. As such, the audience for this platform includes undergraduate students, postgraduates and industrial chemists: anyone who is involved, or could be involved, in practical pharmaceutical chemistry.

The intention is that the material here is a permanent resource that can be studied in various ways:

  • as a course in its own right
  • embedded into other courses, in whole or part
  • as individual stand-alone modules that can be chosen to suit the needs of individuals or organisations

The educational material is licensed (unless otherwise specified) under Creative Commons license CC BY-NC 4.0, which gives permission for it to be shared and adapted for non-commercial purposes as long as attribution is given.

The full list of available courses and modules is available from the home page.
